# How to Add Your Artifact to the Museum

1. Fork this repository (click the Fork button at the top right).
2. In your forked copy, open `index.html`.
3. Find the `<div id="exhibits">` section.
4. Add a new `<div class="exhibit">` with your information. Example:
   ```html
   <div class="exhibit">
       <h3><a href="https://your-username.github.io/your-repo-name">Your Name's Artifact</a></h3>
       <p>Curator's note: [a short summary of your restoration]</p>
   </div>
